Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Gestura?

Gestura is a smart app that lets you control your computer using your hand movements — no mouse or keyboard needed! Just move your hands in front of your camera to do everyday tasks.

Does Gestura support multiple Operating Systems?

Yes, Gestura is compatible with most OSs. Check our compatibility list for more details. Learn More

Do I need any extra device?

No! You only need your computer and a webcam. Gestura works with most built-in or USB cameras.

Can I use Gestura without the internet?

Yes! Once you’ve installed it, Gestura works completely offline.

Is my camera always recording?

No. Gestura doesn’t record or save any video. It only looks at your hand movements in real time — all safely inside your own computer.

What should I do if Gestura is not detecting my gestures?

Ensure your camera is properly configured and that there is adequate lighting. Refer to our troubleshooting guide for more tips. Learn More

Can I suggest new features?

Of course! We love new ideas. You can share your suggestions from inside the app or on our official website. Or suggest here.
